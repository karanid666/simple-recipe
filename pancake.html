Fluffy Buttermilk Pancakes

These pancakes rise tall thanks to a buttermilk and baking soda combo.Mix gently for tender results.

Ingredients

  • Dry
    • All-purpose flour — 1½ cups
    • Sugar — 2 tbsp
    • Baking powder — 2 tsp
    • Baking soda — ½ tsp
    • Salt — ¼ tsp
  • Wet
    • Buttermilk — 1¼ cups
    • Egg — 1 large
    • Unsalted butter (melted) — 2 tbsp
    • Vanilla — 1 tsp
  • For Cooking — neutral oil or butter

Steps

  1. Whisk dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Whisk wet ingredients in another bowl;pour into dry and fold until just combined.
  3. Heat a griddle over medium;lightly grease.
  4. Pour ¼-cup scoops; cook until bubbles form and edges set, then flip and cook through.
  5. Serve warm with butter and maple syrup.

Tip:Rest the batter 5-10 minutes for even fluffier pancakes.
