Chana Masala

Hearty chickpeas simmered with onions,tomatoes,and warm spices.Its weeknight-friendly and meal-prep gold.

Ingredients

  • Aromatics
    • Onion — 1 medium, finely chopped
    • Garlic — 3 cloves, minced
    • Ginger — 1 inch, grated
  • Spices
    • Cumin seeds — 1 tsp
    • Coriander — 2 tsp
    • Turmeric — ½ tsp
    • Garam masala — 1 tsp
    • Chili powder — to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil; sizzle cumin seeds 30 seconds.
  2. Cook onion until golden; add garlic and ginger for 1 minute.
  3. Stir in spices; toast briefly, then add tomatoes and cook to a jammy sauce.
  4. Add chickpeas and water; simmer 10-15 minutes until slightly thickened.
  5. Finish with lemon juice and garam masala; adjust salt and chili.

Tip:For creamier texture, mash a few chickpeas against the pot while simmering.
