Weeknight Spaghetti

This fast marinara leans on pantry staples and a quick simmer for fresh flavor.Its weeknight-friendly,kid-approved,and scales easily.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti — 300 g
  • Olive oil — 2 tbsp
  • Garlic — 3 cloves, thinly sliced
  • Red pepper flakes — pinch
  • Crushed tomatoes — 1 can (400 g)
  • Fresh basil — small handful
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Optional toppings
    • Grated Parmesan
    • Extra virgin olive oil drizzle

Steps

  1. Boil a large pot of salted water; cook spaghetti until al dente.
  2. Meanwhile, warm olive oil in a pan; gently cook garlic and red pepper until fragrant (1-2 min).
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes; simmer 8-10 min. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Serve hot with Parmesan and a drizzle of olive oil.

Tip:If sauce thickens too much, loosen with a splash of pasta water.
